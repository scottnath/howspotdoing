Cannabis is 40% legal in Arkansas

Last reviewed: January 4, 2026

Is it legal for adults to possess cannabis in the state of Arkansas?

NO

""Except as provided by this chapter, it is unlawful for a person to possess a controlled substance."" - https://arkleg.state.ar.us/Acts/CodeSectionsAmended?ddBienniumSession=2015/2015R

Is it legal for adults to buy cannabis in a store in the state of Arkansas?

NO

Is it legal for adults to grow cannabis in the state of Arkansas?

NO

Is it legal for medical marijuana patients to possess cannabis in the state of Arkansas?

YES

"A qualifying patient or designated caregiver in actual possession of a registry identification card shall not be subject to arrest, prosecution, or penalty in any manner, or denied any right or privilege, including but not limited to civil penalty or disciplinary action by a business or occupational or professional licensing board or bureau, for the medical use of marijuana in accordance with this amendment… Qualified registered patients are allowed to purchase up to 2.5 ounces from a dispensary in a 14-day period." - https://healthy.arkansas.gov/programs-services/data-statistics-registries/medical-marijuana/faqs/

Is it legal for medical marijuana patients to buy cannabis in a store in the state of Arkansas?

YES

""Qualified registered patients are allowed to purchase up to 2.5 ounces from a dispensary in a 14-day period."" - https://www.healthy.arkansas.gov/programs-services/topics/medical-marijuana

Is it legal for medical marijuana patients to grow cannabis in the state of Arkansas?

NO

"No. Qualifying registered patients and their designated caregivers cannot grow or cultivate medical marijuana." - https://www.healthy.arkansas.gov/programs-services/topics/medical-marijuana-faqs
