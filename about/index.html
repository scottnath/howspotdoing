How the Legality of cannabis is calculated

We confirm legality on government sites, then score access: 80 for recreational, 30 for medical-only. Store purchase and home cultivation each add 10 points if allowed.

80% Recreational legal
+10% Can buy in stores
+10% Can grow at home

— or if recreational isn't legal —

30% Medical only
+10% Patients can buy
+10% Patients can grow

100% = full recreational access
50% = best medical-only
0% = fully illegal
